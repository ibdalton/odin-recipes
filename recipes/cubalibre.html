Cuba Libre

Teddy Roosevelt's favorite... see if this one brings out the rough rider inside you!

Immerse yourself in the lively spirit of Cuba with the iconic Cuba Libre cocktail. This classic drink, also known as a Rum and Coke with a twist, is a delightful fusion of smooth rum, effervescent cola, and a squeeze of fresh lime. Originating during the early 20th century in Havana, Cuba, the Cuba Libre has become a symbol of relaxation and tropical enjoyment. The combination of rich, aged rum and the crisp, carbonated notes of cola creates a refreshing harmony that is perfect for any occasion.

To craft a perfect Cuba Libre, start with high-quality rum, preferably a golden or dark variety, and pair it with your favorite cola. Squeeze a wedge of lime over the concoction, and enjoy the resulting symphony of flavors that dance on your palate. Whether you're lounging on the beach or celebrating with friends, the Cuba Libre is a timeless cocktail that captures the vibrant essence of Cuba's cultural legacy.

Ingredients:

  • 2.5oz of Plantation Original Dark Rum
  • .75 oz Lime Juice
  • Your Cola of choice
  • Lime Wedge (for garnish)

Directions:

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice
  2. Pour the lime juice and rum into the glass
  3. Top the glass off with the cola
  4. Stir and garnish with a lime wedge
  5. Serve with a straw - preferable metal

And there you have it - a Cuba Libre!
This drink takes the rum and coke - a typical slimy college bar drink - to the next level.
