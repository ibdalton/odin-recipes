Zombie Cocktail

Unleash the undead with the powerful and flavorful Zombie cocktail!

Step into the mysterious realm of Tiki mixology with the Zombie cocktail, a potent and exotic concoction that has become a legend in the world of tropical drinks. Originating in the 1930s, the Zombie is known for its complex blend of rums, fruit juices, and secret ingredients, creating a drink that is both alluring and powerful. This Tiki classic is not for the faint of heart but promises a thrilling journey for adventurous cocktail enthusiasts.

Crafted with various rums, such as light, dark, and overproof varieties, the Zombie is complemented by citrusy notes from lime and grapefruit juices. Don't forget the distinctive flavors of falernum, grenadine, and bitters that contribute to its mysterious charm. Shake well with ice and strain into a glass filled with crushed ice, creating a chilling and exhilarating experience that will transport you to a Tiki paradise.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Light Rum
  • 1 oz Dark Rum
  • 1 oz Overproof Rum
  • ¾ oz Lime Juice
  • ½ oz Grapefruit Juice
  • ½ oz Falernum
  • ½ oz Grenadine
  • 1 dash Angostura Bitters

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well to chill the mixture.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with crushed ice.
  4. Garnish creatively with a slice of citrus or mint sprig.
  5. Enjoy responsibly and beware of the Zombie's bite!
