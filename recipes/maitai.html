Maitai

Pay respects to the original! A strong rum cocktail with hints of citrus, almond, and mint.

Indulge in the iconic Trader Vic's original Maitai, a timeless cocktail that captures the essence of tropical bliss. This classic recipe combines the richness of premium rum with the vibrant notes of fresh citrus, almond, and a touch of invigorating mint. Crafted with precision and heritage, Trader Vic's Maitai is more than just a drink; it's a celebration of exotic flavors that transport you to the sun-soaked shores of the Pacific.

Experience the art of cocktail perfection with this renowned recipe. Whether you're hosting a gathering or simply unwinding after a long day, Trader Vic's Maitai brings a taste of paradise to your glass. Follow our easy steps below to recreate the magic and savor the distinctive blend of ingredients that make Trader Vic's Maitai a cherished classic in the world of mixology.

Ingredients:

  • 2oz of Dark Jamaican Rum
  • .5oz Simple Syrup
  • .5oz Liquid Alchemist Orgeat
  • .5oz Pierre Ferrand Dry Curacao
  • .75 oz Lime Juice
  • Sprig of Mint (for garnish)

Directions:

  1. Add the rum, simple syrup, orgeat, curacao, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice
  2. Close the shaker and shake until frost develops on the outside
  3. Fill a maitai glass with crushed pebble ice
  4. Pour the shaker's contents into the maitai glass, straining the ice from the shaker
  5. Clap the sprig of mint in the palm of your hand then stick it into the glass
  6. Place one of the squeezed lime halves into the maitai glass with the rind facing up

And there you have it - an original Maitai!
Notice how the lime garnish looks like an island in the tropics - and the mint a lonely tree - making this a lush tropical experience on all of the senses.
Be sure to smell the mint while you sip on this drink for the full experience!
